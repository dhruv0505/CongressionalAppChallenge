Our Mission

We strive to educate our users and enforce healthy habits that benefit our environments. Our goal is to be a fun and usable app to evoke the competition for self improvement among peers.

Experience the joy, drive to improve, desire to help our planet, and so much more by using our app.

Why and How?

It is commonly known that our planet is falling into a state of despair and it is nescessary that we inspire other to act on this.

By creating an app that gives you challenges for multipls weeks, we hope that these "challenges" will become a common part of your schedule even without the app.
